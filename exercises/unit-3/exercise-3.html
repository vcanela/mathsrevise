Worked Examples

Example 1: Corresponding angles (F-shape)

When a transversal crosses two parallel lines, corresponding angles are in the same position at each intersection. They are equal.

Corresponding angles (F-shape): equal. Look for angles in the same position at each parallel line.

Example 2: Alternate angles (Z-shape)

Alternate angles are on opposite sides of the transversal, between the parallel lines. They are equal.

Alternate angles (Z-shape): equal. They are between the parallel lines, on opposite sides of the transversal.

Example 3: Co-interior angles (C-shape)

Co-interior angles are on the same side of the transversal, between the parallel lines. They sum to 180°.

Co-interior angles (C-shape, also called allied angles): sum to 180°. They are on the same side of the transversal.
Memory trick: F = equal (Corresponding)  ·  Z = equal (Alternate)  ·  C = sum to 180° (Co-interior)
