Example 2: Key angle rules

Rule 1 Complementary angles sum to 90°. Example: 34° and 56° are complementary because 34 + 56 = 90.

Rule 2 Supplementary angles sum to 180°.

Rule 3 Angles on a straight line sum to 180°. All angles at a point on one side of a line add up to 180°.

Rule 4 Angles around a point sum to 360°.

Key Rules:
Complementary angles sum to 90°  ·  Supplementary angles sum to 180°  ·  Angles on a straight line sum to 180°  ·  Angles around a point sum to 360°
