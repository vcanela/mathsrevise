Worked Examples

Example 1: Addition: 5.34 + 2.78

Step 1 Line up the decimal points.

5 . 3 4 + 2 . 7 8 8 . 1 2

Step 2 Add each column right to left: 4+8=12 (write 2, carry 1), 3+7+1=11 (write 1, carry 1), 5+2+1=8.

Answer: 8.12

Example 2: Subtraction: 9.61 − 4.37

Step 1 Line up the decimal points.

9 . 6 1 − 4 . 3 7 5 . 2 4

Step 2 Subtract right to left: 1−7 (borrow → 11−7=4), 6−1−3=2, 9−4=5.

Answer: 5.24
